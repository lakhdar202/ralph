### Algorithm Details:
The TOPSIS (Technique for Order of Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution) implementation follows these steps:
1. **Build Decision Matrix**: Extract candidate values for each attribute
2. **Normalize**: Vector normalization (value / sqrt(sum of squares))
3. **Apply Weights**: Multiply normalized values by attribute weights
4. **Find Ideal Solutions**: 
   - A+ (best): max for beneficial, min for non-beneficial attributes
   - A- (worst): min for beneficial, max for non-beneficial attributes
5. **Calculate Distances**: Euclidean distance from each candidate to A+ and A-
6. **Closeness Score**: D- / (D+ + D-) â€” higher = better
7. **Rank**: Sort by closeness score descending
